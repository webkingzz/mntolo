Founder of Kriesi.at
Kriesi.at - Premium WordPress Themes

Kriesi.at was founded in 2006 as a personal blog by Christian Budschedl (aka Kriesi). During his education at  SAE Vienna, a private college teaching web design & development, the personal blog transformed into a professional blog, covering HTML, CSS, Photoshop and ultimately WordPress topics.

Shortly after this first transformation, Kriesi started releasing WordPress Themes at Themeforest, converting the site once again by adding a theme catalogue. As the site grew from blog to business site, the team grew as well.

Support requests started to roll in and during the following years we added additional members to cover those support requests and speed up development. We are still only a small team but we really like it that way. It allows us to stay flexible, easily respond to trends and offer the best customer support imaginable :)
