    Ismael
    Moderator

    Hi,

    Thank you for the update.

    We revert the modifications in the loop-index.php file to bring back the featured image. And to prevent duplication in the lightbox, we removed the “post-entry” and “#main” selectors in the lightbox group parameter, then replaced them with “entry_content” and “blog_meta”. You’ll find the modification in the js > avia-snippet-lightbox.js file:

    groups			:	['.entry-content', '.blog-meta', '.avia-slideshow', '.avia-gallery', '.av-horizontal-gallery', '.av-instagram-pics', '.portfolio-preview-image', '.portfolio-preview-content', '.isotope', '.sidebar', '.main_menu', '.<a href='https://refer.wordpress.com/r/84/woocommerce/' target='_blank' rel="nofollow">woocommerce</a>-product-gallery'], 
			autolinkElements:   '.small-preview, .entry-content a[href$=jpg], a.lightbox, a[rel^="prettyPhoto"], a[rel^="lightbox"], a[href$=jpg], a[href$=png], a[href$=gif], a[href$=jpeg], a[href*=".jpg?"], a[href*=".png?"], a[href*=".gif?"], a[href*=".jpeg?"], a[href$=".mov"] , a[href$=".swf"] , a:regex(href, .vimeo\.com/[0-9]) , a[href*="youtube.com/watch"] , a[href*="screenr.com"], a[href*="iframe=true"]',

    Best regards,
    Ismael
