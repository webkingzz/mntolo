    Hi,
    Sorry for the late reply, that is good news, Yes Enfold can handle hundreds of products, many of our users have large stores.
    As for Woocommerce, Woocommerce comes with a store front and product pages, but Woocommerce restricts some of the aspects of these. You can design your own storefront and product page with our “advanced layout builder” elements (blocks) or you could use Gutenberg blocks, but I’m not sure what blocks are available, so I’m not sure how that would look. Please take a look at our Shop Demo to see what can be done with our advanced layout builder, it also has a full-screen slider. I don’t have a demo of the Gutenberg blocks & Woocommerce.
    Also take a look at all of our demos, you can pretty much max & match any of these elements (blocks)
    But I must say that we do have a couple of issues with Gutenberg that we are working on, and our builder is not designed to switch back and forth with Gutenberg on the same page. But you can build pages and post with either or on the same site.
    Most people have disabled Gutenberg and build with our advanced layout builder, but it’s up to you.
    I hope I have answered your questions.
