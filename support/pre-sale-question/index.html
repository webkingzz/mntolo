what do you want to know?

Got questions regarding any of our themes? Feel free to use the form beside. Your request will then be posted to the pre-sale section of our forum. You can ask your question in English or German but since we got more English speaking moderators, you will receive a faster answer when using English ;)

Please note that theme support is not provided by email and is offered by forum only.

Any questions sent via mail that could also be posted in the forum will receive no reply. We are sorry for that, but its otherwise impossible for us to verify that you are a legit customer :)
